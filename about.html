The Legacy of Sethsuwa Pranajeewa

Sethsuwa Pranajeewa medicine is the most sought-after product at Sethsuwa Hospital. It is trusted and widely used both in Sri Lanka and the world over. The miraculous formula was originated by the Buddhist monk, Venerable Waga Gnanaloka Thero, who first treated hundreds of patients with his traditional medicine, obtained from ancient Ola leaf inscriptions passed down through generations.

Even at eighty years of age, the monk looked as youthful as forty. More importantly, he led an exceptionally active life, waking up at 4:30 a.m. daily to devote his early hours to paying reverence to Lord Buddha. The hours that followed were spent in compassionate, free treatment for his patients.

Hundreds gathered daily at the Sethsuwa Ayurveda Hospital, then affectionately called the "Veda Madura" (Ayurveda House), where an initial spoonful of Sethsuwa Pranajeewa was administered to every single patient. News reached far and wide across the entire country of the tremendous, immediate relief that followed the administration of that first tablespoon.

"Passed down through ancient Ola leaves dating back over 200 years, Sethsuwa Pranajeewa remains a timeless cure across generations."

The monk's only understudy was Dr. Sujeewa Vithanage, a brilliant young doctor with a medical degree from the University of Colombo (BAMS), who was engaged in extensive research into ancient medical systems. Together, they prepared the miraculous medicine in massive traditional cauldrons.

Following the passing of Venerable Waga Gnanaloka, Dr. Sujeewa took over the sacred responsibility of crafting this ancient recipe. For over two centuries, Sethsuwa Pranajeewa served as the sole, trusted cure for villagers afflicted with various ailments, providing relief to grandfathers, fathers, and children alike.

Today, there are hundreds of loyal users of Sethsuwa Pranajeewa medicine in the United Kingdom, USA, and other parts of the world. Among the countless heartening experiences shared globally, an American doctor revealed that after facing prostate cancer surgery and experiencing his PSA readings doubling every three months, taking Sethsuwa Pranajeewa alongside his radiation treatment brought miraculous improvements.
